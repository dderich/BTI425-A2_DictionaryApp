About Page

The app’s purpose is to define or translate the English-language terminology we use in the computer programming courses in the School of SDDS, for use by all students, whether or not English is a student’s first language.

The general functionality of the user app includes:

  1. View and browse a list of all terms
  2. Search for a term in English
  3. View details about a term, and its meaning in English and in other languages
  4. Add a new term, English, or in another language (including its first or initial definition)
  5. For an existing term (English or other), add another definition/data
